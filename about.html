ABOUT US

Polyset Industries Pvt. Ltd. (PIPL) was established in 1981 with ‘Modern’s Quality Products’ as their brand that produced plastic water sets and mugs. The company, over the years, expanded the brand and now boasts a product portfolio of more than 100 products. We operate in a factory which spans over 50,000 m2 of covered area located in one of the largest industrial areas of Karachi. With its vast experience of over 40 years in this industry, PIPL has an extensive pool of experienced labor force and technically superior machinery. PIPL is a renowned name in the Pakistani plastic industry because of its quality and availability across the country at all times. PIPL products which attract increasingly greater attention in Pakistan are notably exported to countries including UK, UAE and Afghanistan. We have a strict policy regarding customer satisfaction and that is exactly why we keep updating our product portfolio regularly so our customers can have new and updated products available for their use. PIPL has a broad marketing network by way of wholesale dealers and market chains in all regions of Pakistan hence being successfully available at every plastic store in Pakistan in a short time. PIPL takes pride in its effective stock control and dispatch system for reaching consumers at every point in Pakistan.
